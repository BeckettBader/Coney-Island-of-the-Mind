Dreams of Screens

In the city that whispers in instagram feeds,

Where dreams are cradled in neon beams,

York breathes, its heart in rhythmic trance,

Memories flicker, igniting chance.

Under the moonlit sky, memories entwine,

With the glowing screens, a digital shrine,

York, a canvas, painted in time's embrace,

Where past and present in circuitry trace.

Each street corner, a story, a digital mind,

Preserving moments, leaving none behind,

Here, the pulse of innovation never wanes,

In the dance of epochs, technology reigns.

The Code of the Streets

Binary beats underfoot, the city's heart,

Where glass towers rise and cables part,

Each street whispers in data streams,

A symphony of tech-born dreams.

Addiction

Each day, a dance of fingers on a screen,

A swipe, a tap, a constant craving for more,

An addiction, so subtle, yet so keen,

With every click, our souls become so sore.

In subway cars, heads bow, eyes locked away,

Lost in a virtual realm, we've come to dwell,

The real world fades, as pixels start to sway,

Our human connections, we bid them farewell.

Oh, how we've traded touch for text and tweets,

As technology's grip on us completes.
